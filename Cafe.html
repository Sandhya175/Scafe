Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our cafe is open from 8:00am to 12:30am every day.


Do you offer takeout or delivery services?

Yes, we offer both takeout and delivery services. You can place orders through our website or contact us directly.


Is there Wi-Fi available for customers?

Certainly! We provide complimentary Wi-Fi for our customers.


Do you have options for customers with dietary restrictions, such as gluten-free or vegan choices?

Absolutely. Our menu includes gluten-free and vegan options. Please ask our staff for assistance in choosing the right dish for your dietary needs.


Are there any loyalty programs or discounts for regular customers?

Yes, we have a loyalty program that rewards our regular customers with discounts and special offers. Joining is easy, just ask our staff for details.


Do you have outdoor seating available?

Yes, we have outdoor seating options for customers who prefer to enjoy their coffee in the open air.
